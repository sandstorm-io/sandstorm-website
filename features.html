Core Features

  • apps and growing

    New apps are being added to the Sandstorm App Market every week covering a wide range of use cases.

    We have apps for document editing, chat, task management, file storage, email, music playing, code hosting, forums, games, and more. Check them out on the Sandstorm App Market.

  • Single Sign-on

    Once you sign into Sandstorm, all your apps know who you are. No need to sign in to each one individually and remember dozens of passwords.

  • Unified sharing access control

    You can share any Sandstorm "grain" (such as a document or a chat room) with your friends or teammates using one common sharing interface.

  • Connect apps with Powerbox

    Create a survey form that feeds into a spreadsheet, attach a document to a chat message, or give a scheduling app access to your calendar. All these interactions are handled through Sandstorm's "Powerbox" UI, which lets apps find each other and lets you grant permissions easily and securely.

Security Features

  • Protection against app bugs

    Sandstorm implements a wide range of measures designed to protect against security bugs in apps. Most bugs that would be serious security problems on other platforms either don't matter at all on Sandstorm or have drastically reduced impact.

    Read our docs to learn how it works.

  • Sandboxing and confinement

    A Sandstorm app cannot talk to the rest of the world unless you give it permission. That means you can put sensitive secrets in any app and be rest-assured that your data is safe. It also means that apps cannot "phone home" to their developers in order to spy on you.

  • Auditable access

    You can always see who has access to your grains -- including people with whom you shared and other grains to which you connected it -- and you can revoke these connections at any time.

  • Authentic apps

    When you install an app on Sandstorm, you can see exactly who created the app package, including such details as their verified Twitter and GitHub accounts, verified domain names, and their PGP key fingerprint.

  • Automatic, authentic updates

    Just like your web browser, it's important to keep Sandstorm up-to-date. Self-hosted Sandstorm updates itself automatically in-place with no action needed by you. All updates are cryptographically signed and verified.

More

Here are even more features available in all versions of Sandstorm

  • Tabbed UI

    Quickly see and switch between your open grains.

  • Backups

    You can download a .zip backup of any grain with one click. You can also automatically backup all your data to the cloud, encrypted.

  • Notifications

    Notifications from all apps are collected in one place, much like on your phone.

  • Mentions

    Many apps, such as Wekan and Rocket.Chat, allow you to @-mention other users like you would on Twitter or GitHub. @-mentioning optionally notifies the target and shares with them.

  • Search

    Search across all your data from all apps in one place.

  • Connected Identities

    Connect your Sandstorm account to multiple external identities, such as Twitter or GitHub accounts, so that people can share with you using the identity they already know you by.
