Features for Organizations

  • On-prem deployment

    Get the ease-of-use of SaaS while keeping your data in-house. Deploy to physical machines or any private or public cloud.

  • LDAP, SAML and Active Directory integration

    Let employees log in with their company-wide credentials.

  • Global Access Control

    Set organization-wide access control policies, such as prohibiting your employees from sharing grains outside of the organization. (Coming soon.)

  • Global Audit Logging

    Monitor data across your whole organization to keep track of who has accessed what. (Coming soon.)

Blackrock: Sandstorm at Scale

In development. See the code »

    Blackrock is an alternative back-end for Sandstorm designed for scale. It is the technology we use to run Sandstorm Oasis.

  • Automatic Scale-out

    Scale out free machines to thousands as your needs grow.

  • Automatic Failover

    Random hardware failures are invisible to your users.

  • Automatic Encryption

    Every grain is encrypted at rest with a unique key which can only be accessed by those with whom the grain is shared. (Coming soon.)
